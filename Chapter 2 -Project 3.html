Doraemon Family

by Takashi Murakami

The Doraemon Family was painted in 2016 by the Japnese artist Takashi Murakami , and today hangs in many famous museums. It is the third of three portraits of the Murakami family the artist painted that year.

Date: 2016
Medium: Oil on Canvas
Dimensions: 100 x 70 cm
Home: Tokyo, Japan;
